Who I Am

Hi, I'm Jennifer Ford. I am a Graphic Designer, currently working as a Multimedia Developer for the University of Florida's Career Resource Center.

I am also a current graduate student at the University of Florida College of Journalism and Communications, studying Web Design and Online Communications. I have a B.A. in Mass Communications with an emphasis in Graphic Design & Visual Communications from Idaho State University with a minor in Fine Arts/Studio Art.

Former graphic designer for the New York Times Syndicate, primarily working on the New York Times International Weekly and Spending Well and Chair of the NYT Editing Center's Employee Committee.

Skilled in InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, HTML and CSS, Audience Development, SEO and social media. I am also an experienced illustrator, photographer, printmaker and painter who always enjoys experimenting with new mediums and dabbling in sculpture.
